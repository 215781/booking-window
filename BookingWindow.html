Signal Guide

What each signal means

Book Now

"The window is open. Conditions are in your favour."

Price has moved to a historically favourable level, availability is tightening, or both. The data suggests this combination has reached its optimal booking point. Acting now is rational.

Watch

"Something is shifting. Worth revisiting within days."

We are observing meaningful movement in price or availability — but the picture isn't yet clear enough to signal either way. Check back in the next few days as the pattern resolves.

Hold

"Stable conditions. No urgency in either direction."

Price is stable and availability is comfortable relative to historical norms. There's no compelling reason to act immediately. We'll alert you if conditions change.

The Method

How Booking Window works

01

Every major ski resort. Every Saturday departure across the season. Standard party sizes. We've been building this price history since autumn 2025 — and it compounds in value with every passing week.

02

A raw price is just a number. Context is what makes it actionable. We measure price direction, velocity of change, and availability tightening — the signals that Club Med's own site cannot give you.
