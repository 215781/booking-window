Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When To Book collects only the email address you provide when signing up for price alerts. We do not collect names, payment details, or any other personal information.

How we use it

Your email address is used solely to send you Club Med ski price alerts for the resorts and dates you requested. We will not use it for marketing, and we will not share it with third parties.

Cookies and tracking

This site uses no tracking cookies and no third-party analytics. We use a single local storage entry to remember that you dismissed the cookie notice — this contains no personal data and never leaves your browser.

Price data

Prices displayed on this site are sourced from publicly available Club Med pricing. When To Book is an independent price intelligence tool and is not affiliated with Club Med.

Your rights

To remove your email address from our alerts list, contact us at hello@whentobook.co.uk and we will delete it promptly.
